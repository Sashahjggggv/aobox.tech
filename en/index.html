Services
Consultation
Our company provides introductory consulting advice to interested potential investors who want to receive passive income from real estate.
Investment management
Investment management is based on full control of all processes that are required for the effective and legally established course of the whole process.
Construction
The construction industry in the Aobox group is managed by the company "DANĚ A FINANCE, spol. S r.o." which is in the market since 2003, with the provision of services in construction. The company owns machinery, auxiliary equipment, which creates a smooth flow of all stages of construction. Workers with 15-20 years of experience in construction.
Selling
In order for the whole process of investing in real estate to be completed, the built real estate goes to the sale market, where the final customer is provided with a full range of services for the acquisition of property rights. When selling real estate at the construction stage, the process of change from the end buyer is managed much faster in our case, than when each stage is managed by unrelated companies.
